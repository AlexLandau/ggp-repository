At each step, each player chooses a cell in the grid that has not yet been chosen. Each cell that
the Crosser picks becomes part of a bridge from the left side to the right side, which can go along
diagonals but cannot backtrack. The Crosser if they can succeed at building such a bridge.
Each cell that the Blocker picks becomes a gap. The Blocker wins if the Crosser cannot build their
bridge.
